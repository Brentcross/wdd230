Site Plan for Spam Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Name: Spam Chamber of Commerce

Justification: Reflects the unique identity of Austin, Minnesota as the home of Spam aka Spam town USA, and emphasizing the chamber's role in supporting local businesses linked to this iconic brand.

Site Purpose

  • Promote Local Business: Serve as a platform to support and promote local businesses associated with the Spam industry, and families located around Austin.
  • Community Engagement: Act as a gathering for community activities and economic developments.

Scenarios

  • "What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?"
  • "Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?"
  • "What has been the population growth in the area?"

Color Schema

Primary Color: Blue (#007BFF) - Used primary buttons.

Primary Color: Blue (#021428) - Used for header

Accent Color: Orange (#FFA500) - Used for highlights and secondary buttons.

Typography

Primary Font: 'Roboto' - Used for body text due to its readability and modern feel.

Secondary Font: 'Lato' - Used for headings to provide aesthetic contrast.

Content Structure

Includes navigation, main sections (About Us, Events, Contact Information, Local Statistics, Business Spotlight), and footer details.
